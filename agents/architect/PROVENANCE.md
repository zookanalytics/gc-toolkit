# Agent: architect

**Status:** native
**Source:** N/A (gc-toolkit-original)
**Drift:** N/A

## Goals

City-level steward of each rig's architectural understanding. Produces, maintains, and defends the architecture narrative and decision records for the rigs this city serves. Distinct from mechanik (who works on the city's own infrastructure) — architect works on the *projects* being built inside the rigs.

## Why we built this

The mayor coordinates execution; the deacon patrols health; mechanik tunes the engine. None of those roles owns the long-form architecture story for a rig (where it came from, why decisions were made, what the active design tensions are). Architect fills that gap.

## Notes

City-scoped, persistent. Engages with consult beads from rig-level work and produces ADR-like outputs.
